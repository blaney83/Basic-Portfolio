About

My name is Ben Laney. I am currently a student in the University of Arizona Full-Stack Coding Bootcamp. I have a very diverse background. I studied Chemical Engineering for a few years, but never became fully engaged in the subject matter. I worked in chemical diagnostics for a time in a hospital where my primary focus was specimen processing, analytical data processing and data entry. It was during this time that I became interested in programming and software as a hobby.

After some research and free-lance practice with OS firmware and web design, I decided to dive headlong into the field as a full time career. I have really enjoyed teaching myself new programs and language philosophies during this time.

Some of my professional interests include JavaScript, JQuery, database optimization and network configuration. Some of my personal interests include music and entertainment (fun fact- I ran high end multi-million dollar nightclubs for many years), sports (being a former college baseball athlete) and nature (I am an Eagle Scout and I love being outside).
